# CCL Model Recommendation Engine — Workflow Questions

This document lists every question and prompt shown to users across the recommendation workflow. Please review and flag any that feel unclear, confusing, or that you'd like to reword.

---

## Step 1 — School Context

### "Tell us about your community"

**Prompt heading:** *Tell us about your community*

**Consider addressing:**
1. What does your student community look like? (demographics, background, needs)
2. Are there any policy considerations or mandates that shape this experience?
3. What existing industry or employer partnerships are relevant to this initiative?
4. Are there post-secondary institutions connected to what you're designing?
5. What is unique about your context that we should understand?

---

## Step 2 — Aims for Learners

### Outcomes Context

**Prompt heading:** *Anything else about your outcomes? Share any context that helps us understand why these outcomes matter for the experience you're designing.*

**Consider addressing:**
1. Why are these outcomes the most important for the experience you're designing?
2. What student needs or goals led you to these choices?
3. How do these connect to the broader vision for this initiative?
4. Are there outcomes you're still debating or want to learn more about?

---

### LEAPs Context

**Prompt heading:** *Anything else about your LEAPs? Share additional context about the learning experience and culture you're trying to create.*

**Consider addressing:**
1. What kind of learning culture or environment should this experience create?
2. Why are these learning principles important for this initiative?
3. Are there tensions or trade-offs between your LEAPs worth flagging?
4. What would students say about this experience if you succeeded?

---

## Step 3 — Learning Experience & Practices

### Practices Context

**Prompt heading:** *Anything else about your practices? Share any context that helps us understand why these practices matter for this experience.*

**Consider addressing:**
1. Why are these practices the right fit for the experience you're designing?
2. Are there practices you're already using that you want to deepen or expand?
3. What conditions or supports would help these practices thrive in this initiative?
4. Are there trade-offs or tensions between any of the practices you've chosen?

---

## Step 4 — System Elements

Each category below includes a context screen and, where applicable, structured multiple-choice questions.

---

### Curriculum, Instruction & Assessment

#### Context Screen

**Prompt heading:** *Tell us about your curriculum & assessment context*

**Consider addressing:**
1. Are there any mandated curriculum programs or instructional models you must keep?
2. Are there assessment structures (grading systems, exams, GPA requirements, etc.) that cannot change?
3. Is there anything about your instructional approach that would make certain types of models a non-starter?

*(No multiple-choice questions for this category)*
